About Us

TaskDesk, as the name suggest, is a task managing app. It allows you to effortlessly create tasks and group them into particular category.

TaskDesk believes in giving you a personalized experience. Therefore, it allows you to change how the app displays the tasks. A user can change the colors of different priority level (i.e high,medium and low) according to user's preferences.
